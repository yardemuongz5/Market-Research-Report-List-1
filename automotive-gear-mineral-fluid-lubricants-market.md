<p><p>The Automotive Gear Mineral Fluid Lubricants market is categorized into three main types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants are derived from saturated hydrocarbons, offering excellent stability and low pour points. Naphthenic lubricants contain cycloparaffins, providing superior lubricating properties and better thermal stability. Aromatic lubricants, which contain unsaturated hydrocarbons, offer high solvency but may pose environmental concerns. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing performance, protecting components, and extending the lifespan of automotive gear systems.</p></p>
