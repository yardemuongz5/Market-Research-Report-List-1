<p><p>The thermal imaging equipment market is primarily divided into two types: infrared thermal imaging and microwave thermal imaging. Infrared thermal imaging utilizes infrared radiation to detect heat emitted by objects, enabling temperature assessment and visualization in various applications, including military, medical, and industrial sectors. In contrast, microwave thermal imaging employs microwave radiation to capture thermal profiles, particularly useful in detecting concealed objects and monitoring environmental conditions. Both technologies play vital roles in enhancing safety, efficiency, and accuracy across diverse fields.</p></p>
