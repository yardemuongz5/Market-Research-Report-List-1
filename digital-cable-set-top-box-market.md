<p><p>The Digital Cable Set-Top Box market includes two main types: OTT (Over the Top) and DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission). OTT services deliver content via the internet, bypassing traditional cable platforms, allowing users to stream shows and movies on various devices. In contrast, DTT involves broadcasting digital signals directly to terrestrial antennas, providing access to TV channels without a cable subscription. Both types cater to evolving consumer preferences for flexibility and convenience in content consumption, shaping the future of television viewing.</p></p>
