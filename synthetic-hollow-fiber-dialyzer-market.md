<p><ul><li>Low Flux Membranes</li><li>High Flux Membranes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Synthetic Hollow-Fiber Dialyzer market is primarily segmented into two types: Low Flux Membranes and High Flux Membranes. Low Flux Membranes are designed for patients with stable renal function and typically have smaller pore sizes, filtering out larger molecules more effectively. In contrast, High Flux Membranes feature larger pores, allowing for the removal of a broader range of waste products, making them suitable for patients requiring more intensive dialysis. Both types cater to varying patient needs and clinical scenarios.</p></p>
