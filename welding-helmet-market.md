<p><ul><li>Passive Welding Helmet</li><li>Auto Darkening Welding Helmets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding helmet market comprises two primary types: passive and auto-darkening helmets. Passive welding helmets feature fixed shade lenses, offering protection from UV rays and sparks, but require manual adjustment for different lighting conditions. Auto-darkening helmets, on the other hand, utilize sensors to automatically adjust the lens shade based on the brightness of the welding arc, providing greater convenience and improved visibility. This advancement enhances safety and comfort, appealing to both amateur and professional welders.</p></p>
