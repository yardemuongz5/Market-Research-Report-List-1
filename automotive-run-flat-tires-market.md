<p><strong>The Automotive Run-flat Tires Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-supporting Technology</li><li>Self-sealing Technology</li><li>Auxiliary-supported Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive run-flat tire market comprises three distinct technologies. Self-supporting technology features reinforced sidewalls that maintain tire shape even with low pressure, allowing continued driving for a limited distance. Self-sealing technology incorporates a sealant within the tire to automatically seal small punctures, minimizing air loss. Auxiliary-supported technology relies on an additional support ring within the tire, enabling the vehicle to operate without air pressure. These innovations enhance safety and convenience for drivers by eliminating the immediate need for tire changes.</p></p>
