<p><ul><li>Modified Flame Retardant Fiber</li><li>Essential Flame Retardant Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fire-resistant fabrics market comprises two primary types: Modified Flame Retardant Fiber and Essential Flame Retardant Fiber. Modified Flame Retardant Fibers are engineered textiles that incorporate chemical treatments or additives to enhance their fire-resistant properties, offering flexibility and versatility for various applications. In contrast, Essential Flame Retardant Fibers are inherently flame-resistant materials, such as aramid or modacrylic fibers, that provide reliable protection without the need for chemical treatments. Both types are crucial for industries requiring safety and compliance with fire hazard regulations.</p></p>
