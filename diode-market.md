<p><p>The diode market encompasses several types, each serving distinct applications. Schottky diodes are known for their fast switching speeds and low forward voltage drop, ideal for power applications. TVS diodes protect circuits from voltage spikes. Switching diodes are used in high-speed switching applications. Zener diodes provide voltage regulation, ensuring stable outputs. Rectifier diodes facilitate current flow in one direction, crucial for converting AC to DC. Other types include photodiodes and laser diodes, catering to specialized uses across various industries.</p></p>
