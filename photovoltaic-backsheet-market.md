<p><p>The photovoltaic backsheet market is divided into two main types: fluoropolymer and non-fluoropolymer. Fluoropolymer backsheets are known for their excellent durability, weather resistance, and electrical insulation, making them ideal for high-performance solar panels. In contrast, non-fluoropolymer backsheets, often made from materials like polyester or polyvinyl, are typically more cost-effective and environmentally friendly but may offer lower resistance to harsh conditions. Both types serve essential roles in enhancing the longevity and efficiency of solar energy systems.</p></p>
