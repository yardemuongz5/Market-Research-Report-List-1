<p><strong>The Medical Bone Densitometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Bone Densitometers</li><li>Peripheral Bone Densitometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical bone densitometers market is primarily categorized into two types: axial and peripheral bone densitometers. Axial bone densitometers, typically found in clinical settings, measure bone density in central sites like the spine and hip, providing comprehensive assessments of osteoporosis risk. Peripheral bone densitometers, on the other hand, are portable devices used to assess bone density in peripheral sites such as the wrist or heel. They offer quicker, less expensive screening options but may provide less detailed information compared to axial models.</p></p>
