Electrochemical Type relies on electrochemical reactions to identify gases, offering good selectivity. Conductive Polymer Type employs conductive polymers that change resistance in the presence of specific gases. Photoionizing Type utilizes ionization techniques to detect volatile organic compounds, while Other types may include hybrid or advanced methods.
