<p><p>The Acrylonitrile-Butadiene-Styrene (ABS) resin market is categorized into types like emulsion graft copolymerization and bulk copolymerization, along with others. Emulsion graft copolymerization involves creating ABS in a dispersion, ensuring uniform properties and enhanced toughness. Bulk copolymerization, on the other hand, involves polymerizing the components in a solid-state, resulting in high purity and consistent quality. Other methods may include variations in production techniques or formulations that cater to specific applications, contributing to the versatility of ABS resin in various industries.</p></p>
