<p><strong>The Tumor Detection Kit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ctDNA</li><li>CTC</li><li>Fluid Detection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tumor detection kit market comprises various types, primarily focusing on ctDNA (circulating tumor DNA), CTC (circulating tumor cells), and fluid detection methods. ctDNA analyzes genetic material released from tumors into the bloodstream, providing insights into tumor presence and mutations. CTC targets whole tumor cells circulating in the blood, offering information on cancer progression. Fluid detection encompasses techniques using body fluids, such as urine or saliva, to identify biomarkers indicative of tumors. Together, they enhance early cancer detection and personalized treatment strategies.</p></p>
