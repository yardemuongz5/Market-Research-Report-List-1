<p><p>The dental vacuum systems market can be categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I systems are typically dry vacuum systems that utilize an air compressor to create suction, offering efficient and reliable performance for dental practices. In contrast, Type II systems are wet vacuum systems that use water to create suction, providing a quieter operation and effective debris management but may require more maintenance. Both types cater to different needs in dental practices, ensuring optimal patient care and operational efficiency.</p></p>
