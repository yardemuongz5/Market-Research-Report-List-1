<p><strong>The Cerebrospinal Fluid (CSF) Management Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CSF Shunts</li><li>CSF Drainage Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) management devices are crucial for treating conditions like hydrocephalus and brain injuries. CSF shunts are implanted systems that redirect excess fluid from the brain to another part of the body, often incorporating a valve to regulate flow. CSF drainage systems, on the other hand, provide temporary solutions for fluid removal through external devices, facilitating decompression in acute situations. Both types ensure proper intracranial pressure management and prevent complications related to fluid accumulation.</p></p>
