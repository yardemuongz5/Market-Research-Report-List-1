<p><ul><li>Magnetostrictive</li><li>Piezoelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrasonic cleaning equipment primarily employs two technologies: magnetostrictive and piezoelectric. Magnetostrictive devices utilize a magnetic field to generate ultrasonic waves, offering consistent frequencies and efficient cleaning for larger components. In contrast, piezoelectric technology uses electric fields to create ultrasonic vibrations, ideal for more precise cleaning applications where delicate items require attention. Both types are effective in removing contaminants from various surfaces, enhancing cleaning efficiency across industries such as automotive, electronics, and healthcare.</p></p>
