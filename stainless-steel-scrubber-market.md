<p><strong>The Stainless Steel Scrubber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Winding Scrubbers</li><li>Woven Scrubbers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stainless steel scrubber market is primarily divided into winding scrubbers and woven scrubbers. Winding scrubbers are crafted by coiling thin stainless steel strands, offering flexibility and efficiency for scrubbing pots and pans. They are designed for tough cleaning tasks and maintain their shape. Woven scrubbers, on the other hand, are made by interlacing stainless steel fibers, providing a softer touch while still delivering effective cleaning power. Both types are popular for their durability, rust resistance, and ability to remove stubborn residues.</p></p>
