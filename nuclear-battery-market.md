<p><ul><li>Thermal Conversion Type</li><li>No-Thermal Conversion Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The nuclear battery market can be categorized into two main types: thermal conversion and non-thermal conversion. Thermal conversion types utilize heat generated from radioactive decay to produce electricity, often involving materials that convert heat into usable energy. In contrast, non-thermal conversion types directly convert the energy from radiation into electrical power without the intermediary heat process, utilizing advanced materials and technologies like betavoltaics. Both types offer long-lasting energy solutions with minimal environmental impact, appealing to various applications.</p></p>
