<p><p>The cardiac Holter monitor market is segmented into various types based on the number of channels: 1-channel, 2-channel, 3-channel, and 12-channel devices. The 2-channel and 3-channel monitors capture data from two and three leads, respectively, providing more detailed insights into heart rhythms. The 12-channel monitor offers comprehensive analysis, crucial for diagnosing complex cardiac conditions, making it the most advanced option in the market.</p></p>
