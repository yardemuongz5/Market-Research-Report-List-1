<p><strong>The Decorated Apparel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embroidery</li><li>Screen Printing</li><li>Dye Sublimation</li><li>Direct to Garment Printing</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The decorated apparel market encompasses various printing techniques. Embroidery involves stitching designs directly onto fabric for a textured look. Screen printing uses stencils to push ink through mesh screens, ideal for bold colors and large orders. Dye sublimation infuses dye into fabric, creating vibrant, long-lasting images, commonly used on polyester. Direct to Garment (DTG) printing applies ink directly to textiles using a printer, allowing for detailed designs and lower quantities. Other methods may include heat transfer and vinyl applications, catering to diverse needs.</p></p>
