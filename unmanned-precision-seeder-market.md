<p><p>The unmanned precision seeder market is primarily categorized into two types: Mechanical Drive Type and True Air Suction Type. The Mechanical Drive Type uses a system of gears and levers to plant seeds with high accuracy and control, typically relying on a direct mechanical connection to the planting mechanism. In contrast, the True Air Suction Type employs air pressure to achieve precision placement of seeds, allowing for exceptional seed spacing and depth control, which enhances crop yields and reduces seed waste.</p></p>
